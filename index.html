About Us

Overview

The Morehouse Business Association (MBA), founded in 1980, is one of the AUC's premier student organizations.

The aims of the MBA are to:

  • Help students interested in business develop skills that are necessary for success in their respective careers

  • Provide exposure and information, by way of seminars, workshops, case studies, extra curricular classes and mentor programs, such that students can make informed decisions about their career.

Why MBA?

The MBA focuses on bridging the gap between academia and the workplace. The MBA helps our members obtain successful internships and full-time positions in technology, consulting, venture capital, investment banking, entrepreneurship, advertising, marketing, brand management, and many more!
